How to Donate?

Any donation you make, regardless of the amount, is welcomed and valued by Suicide or Survive.

All donations go directly towards supporting SOS in our mission to challenge the stigma attached to mental health and suicide and allow us to continue to deliver our much-needed workshops and programmes throughout Ireland.
